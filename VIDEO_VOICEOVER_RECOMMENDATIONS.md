## Voice Style Guide for Artists

When contacting voiceover artists, provide this brief:

```
PROJECT: STS Futures Demo Video
DURATION: 2 minutes (244 words)
STYLE: Professional, confident, conversational

TONE GUIDANCE:
- Sound like a knowledgeable friend explaining something exciting
- Authoritative but not stuffy or corporate
- Build energy through the video, peaking at the numbers montage
- Warm and inviting on the closing CTA

REFERENCE VOICES:
- Apple product video narration style
- Stripe/Plaid fintech explainer videos
- Calm confidence, not aggressive sales pitch

TECHNICAL REQUIREMENTS:
- WAV format, 48kHz, 24-bit
- Minimal room noise/echo
- No background music (we'll add separately)
- Separate takes for each scene if possible
```

## Alternative: AI Voiceover (Budget Option)

If budget is extremely limited, consider AI voiceover tools:

| Tool       | Price     | Quality   | Notes                         |
| ---------- | --------- | --------- | ----------------------------- |
| ElevenLabs | $5/month  | Excellent | Most natural AI voices        |
| Murf.ai    | $19/month | Very Good | Good for corporate            |
| Play.ht    | $14/month | Good      | Large voice library           |
| Descript   | $12/month | Good      | Integrated with video editing |

**Pros:** Instant, cheap, unlimited revisions  
**Cons:** Less emotional range, may sound slightly robotic, licensing concerns

**Recommendation:** For a professional product video, invest in a real voiceover artist. The human touch makes a significant difference in perceived quality and trust.
