### Typography

- **Headlines:** Inter Bold or SF Pro Display Bold
- **Metrics:** Tabular numerals (monospace for alignment)
- **Body:** Inter Regular

### Motion Principles

- All movements should feel **smooth and intentional**
- Use **ease-out-cubic** for most transitions
- Camera movements should be **cinematic, not robotic**
- Elements should **breathe** (subtle scale/opacity animations)

## Animated Callout Specifications

Callouts should appear when the camera highlights a metric:

### Callout Design

```
┌─────────────────────────────┐
│  $1,120,525                 │
│  Total Portfolio Value      │
└─────────────────────────────┘
```

### Animation Sequence

1. **Enter:** Slide in from right (0.3s, ease-out)
2. **Hold:** Static for 2-3 seconds
3. **Exit:** Fade out (0.2s)

### Styling

- Background: rgba(0, 212, 255, 0.1) with blur
- Border: 1px solid #00d4ff
- Border radius: 8px
- Text: White, bold for value, regular for label

## Audio Mixing Guide

| Track     | Level             | Notes                         |
| --------- | ----------------- | ----------------------------- |
| Voiceover | -6 dB             | Primary audio, always audible |
| Music     | -18 dB (under VO) | Duck during voiceover         |
| Music     | -12 dB (no VO)    | Bring up during transitions   |
| SFX       | -12 dB            | Subtle, don't overpower       |

### Sound Effects Needed

- Whoosh transitions (3-4 variations)
- UI click sounds (subtle)
- Bass impact for beat hits
- Shimmer/sparkle for CTA button
- Counter tick sound (for number animations)
