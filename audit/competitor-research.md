# Competitor Research - Trading Signal Platforms

## 1. SignalStack
**URL:** https://signalstack.com/
**Focus:** Code-free order automation from any trading platform

### Pricing (Yearly with 50% off first year):
- **Basic:** $135/year (50 signals/month) - ~$11/month
- **Premium:** $485/year (250 signals/month) - ~$40/month  
- **Pro:** $1,735/year (1,000 signals/month) - ~$145/month
- **Free tier:** 5 signals/month

### Key Features:
- 33+ broker integrations (Schwab, IBKR, TradeStation, Tradovate, etc.)
- TradingView webhook integration
- No coding required
- Market, Limit, Stop, Stop-Limit orders
- Crypto, Stocks, Futures, Forex, CFDs, Options
- 0.45 second order execution
- 99.99% uptime
- Unlimited broker connections on all plans
- Paper trading included

### Strengths:
- Very broad broker support
- Simple webhook-based automation
- Clear pricing per signal
- Free tier available

### Weaknesses:
- No strategy performance analytics
- No backtesting data
- Just execution, no strategy creation

## 2. Collective2
**URL:** https://collective2.com/
**Focus:** Strategy marketplace - connect investors with strategy managers

### Business Model:
- Strategy managers create and publish strategies
- Investors subscribe to strategies and auto-trade
- Fixed monthly subscription fees per strategy

### Key Features:
- Strategy marketplace with verified track records
- AutoTrade functionality
- Multiple asset classes (Stocks, Options, Futures)
- Performance tracking and reviews
- Scaling and risk controls

### Strengths:
- Established marketplace with many strategies
- Verified performance records
- Community reviews
- European regulated (CySEC)

### Weaknesses:
- Mixed reviews on Trustpilot
- Some strategies have high drawdowns
- Complex fee structure

## Key Competitive Insights for IntraDay Strategies

1. **Clear pricing tiers** - Most have free tier + multiple paid levels
2. **Broad broker support** - SignalStack has 33+ brokers
3. **Strategy marketplace** - Collective2 lets users browse/compare
4. **Paper trading** - Free simulation before live trading
5. **Webhook simplicity** - Easy TradingView integration

1. **Deep analytics** - Most competitors lack detailed performance analytics
