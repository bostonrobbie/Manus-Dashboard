Refund Policy

Effective Date: December 31, 2025

Our Commitment to You

At STS Futures, we want you to be completely satisfied with your subscription. We offer a straightforward refund policy to ensure you can try our platform with confidence.

1. Free Trial Period

We offer a free tier that allows you to explore the platform before committing to a paid subscription. We encourage all users to take advantage of this free tier to ensure our platform meets their needs before subscribing.

2. Subscription Refund Policy

Our refund policy varies based on your subscription type:

Subscription Type Refund Window Refund Amount
Monthly Subscription Within 7 days of initial purchase Full refund
Yearly Subscription Within 14 days of initial purchase Full refund
After refund window N/A No refund (cancel anytime)

3. Eligibility for Refunds

To be eligible for a refund, you must meet the following criteria:

  • Request the refund within the applicable refund window (7 days for monthly, 14 days for yearly)
  • This must be your first subscription to STS Futures
  • You have not previously received a refund from us
  • Your account has not been terminated for violation of our Terms of Service

4. How to Request a Refund

To request a refund, please follow these steps:

  • Email us at support@stsfutures.com with the subject line "Refund Request"
  • Include your registered email address and the date of purchase
  • Briefly explain the reason for your refund request (optional but helpful)

We will process your refund request within 5-7 business days. Refunds will be credited to the original payment method used for the purchase.

5. Cancellation Policy

You may cancel your subscription at any time through your account settings or by contacting us. Upon cancellation:

  • Your subscription will remain active until the end of your current billing period
  • You will not be charged for subsequent billing periods
  • No partial refunds are provided for unused time after the refund window has passed
  • You will retain access to free tier features after your subscription ends

6. Promotional Offers and Discounts

Subscriptions purchased with promotional codes, discounts, or special offers are subject to the same refund policy. Refunds will be processed based on the actual amount paid after any discounts.

7. Chargebacks

If you believe there has been an error with your billing, please contact us first before initiating a chargeback with your bank or credit card company. We are committed to resolving any billing issues promptly and fairly.

Initiating a chargeback without first contacting us may result in the suspension of your account and may affect your eligibility for future refunds or services.

8. Exceptions

We reserve the right to make exceptions to this policy on a case-by-case basis at our sole discretion. Exceptions may be made for:

  • Technical issues that prevented you from accessing the platform
  • Billing errors on our part
  • Other extenuating circumstances

9. Changes to This Policy

We reserve the right to modify this refund policy at any time. Changes will be effective immediately upon posting to our website. Your continued use of our services after any changes indicates your acceptance of the new policy.

10. Contact Us

If you have any questions about our refund policy, please contact us:

Email: support@stsfutures.com
